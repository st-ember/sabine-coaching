about us

Established in 2001, Coaching Australia is a specialist provider of all aspects of Coaching related services. Our growth to the global stage, serving some 15 + markets saw us establish our international arm, Coach Global in 2014. Our credibility and reputation are evidenced through the longstanding approval, of more than a decade, of our Coach Training offerings by the International Coaching Federation. We tirelessly aim to represent the very best of the Coaching industry in all that we do.
